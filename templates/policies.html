Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Thu 15 Feb, 2024 (02/15/2024) - 19:34 UTC

Welcome to Infomundi. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at contact@infomundi.net or use the contact form.

When you visit our website (https://infomundi.net/), and use our services, you trust us with your personal information. In this privacy policy, we seek to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. We hope you take some time to read through it carefully, as it is important. If there are any terms in this privacy policy that you do not agree with, please discontinue use of our site.

This privacy policy applies to all information collected through our website and related services.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering at the website/posting comments/contacting us. The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the website, the choices you make, and the features you use.

Information Automatically Collected

Some information — such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address and browser and device characteristics — is collected automatically when you visit our website. This information is not stored in our servers nor is used by us, but by third parties such as Cloudflare and Google.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We process your information for purposes based on compliance with our legal obligations, and/or your consent.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We only share information with your consent, or to comply with laws.

4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We keep your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy unless otherwise required by law.

5. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We aim to protect your personal information through a system of organizational and technical security measures.

We employ Argon2, recognized as one of the most robust cryptographic hashing algorithms available, to ensure the utmost security of your passwords. This advanced method ensures that, even in the unlikely event an attacker gains access to our servers, your vital password information remains securely encrypted, safeguarding your privacy and data integrity.

We utilize TLS (Transport Layer Security) to safeguard our online interactions, encrypting the data exchanged between your device and our servers. This ensures that all communications remain confidential and secure, protecting sensitive information from potential cyber threats and unauthorized access.

6. Do We Collect Information from Minors?

We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age.

7. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time. Contact us.

8. Do We Make Updates to This Policy?

Yes, we will update this policy as necessary.

9. Cookies

We only use necessary cookies that are crucial for the proper operation of the website. Third parties (Google and Cloudflare) have cookies on our website for business purposes.

  1. theme (expires in 7 days) – is only created if you change the website theme.
  2. clicked (expires upon browser closure) – is created/updated when you click in a story. This cookie is essential for the webite as we need it for the comments section.
  3. remember_token (expires in 6 months or when user logs out) – is created when you choose to remember your session when you sign in.
  4. infomundi-session (expires upon browser closure) – is created/updated when you view almost every page of this website. Holds information regarding the last country you visited in our website, the last story you clicked, cooldown information for the comments page and a lot more. This cookie is essential for the website to work properly.
  5. cf_clearence – stores the proof of challenge passed. It is used to no longer issue a Cloudflare challenge if present. It is required to reach an origin server.

Community Policy

Last Updated: Thu 15 Feb, 2024 (02/15/2024) - 19:34 UTC

We're thrilled to have you as part of our community. Our goal is to create an inclusive, respectful, and engaging space where all members feel valued and safe. To ensure this, we ask all members to adhere to our Community Content Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this community policy, please contact us at contact@infomundi.net or use the contact form.

1. Respect for All Members

  • Treat all community members with respect.
  • Personal attacks, harassment, bullying, and hate speech are strictly prohibited.
  • Discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, religion, or any other personal characteristic will not be tolerated.

2. Content Guidelines

  • Share content that is relevant and you have the right to distribute, respecting intellectual property rights.
  • Do not post nor share explicit, obscene, or violent content. This inlude links to third parties.
  • Do not share nor promote false information.

3. Engagement Rules

  • All kinds of spamming is prohibited.
  • Please, report any content or behavior that violates our policies.
  • Do not use the platform for illegal activities or to promote dangerous organizations.

4. Privacy and Safety

  • Protect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent.
  • Be mindful of the information you share and its potential impact on others and yourself.

5. Consequences of Violating the Policy

  • Violations may result in a warning, content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent ban, depending on the severity.
  • We reserve the right to take appropriate action to ensure the community remains a safe and welcoming space for all.

6. Changes to the Policy

  • We may update this policy as needed to reflect changes in our community or legal requirements. We encourage members to review it periodically.
  • Changes to the policy will be listed in our changelog.
